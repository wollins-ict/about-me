AI & ICT Strategy for Schools

Technology leadership, built around your school.

Schools deserve technology strategy that is genuinely theirs — grounded in your community, your values, and your educational vision. Not a vendor's template. Not a government-issue framework. Yours. I know the difference because I've built both — and I know which one actually works.

Who this is for
Principals & Heads of School
Who want a trusted perspective on technology decisions — without needing to be technology experts themselves.
Boards & Business Managers
Who need to govern technology risk and investment confidently, with clear advice that connects technology to educational and financial outcomes.
Schools in transition
Who are between technology leaders, managing a team in need of direction, or navigating a change that feels larger than the internal team can absorb.
What I offer
🗺️
ICT Strategy Development

A multi-year technology roadmap built with your leadership team — grounded in your school's strategic plan, your community's needs, and your realistic capacity to deliver it. Something you can govern, not just file.

  • Current state assessment & honest gap analysis
  • Pillar-based strategy co-developed with your team
  • Investment planning & business cases for the board
  • Governance structures that actually get used

AI Governance & Adoption

Practical, values-grounded AI guidance for schools — from policy and parent communication to staff capability programs and responsible adoption. Built for your community, not copied from a generic template.

  • Responsible use policies & acceptable use frameworks
  • Staff capability and professional learning programs
  • Tool evaluation & procurement support
  • Parent & community communication that builds trust
🔒
Cyber Security & Risk

Cyber security that your board can govern and your community can trust — practical programs that address real risk, without the vendor hype or the false sense of security that comes from a single-day training session.

  • Independent cyber security posture assessment
  • Board-level risk reporting that is genuinely useful
  • Staff training & phishing awareness programs
  • Incident response planning & practice
👥
Interim & Advisory Leadership

Experienced technology leadership when and how you need it — covering a gap, supporting a search, mentoring an emerging leader, or providing an independent perspective your existing team can't easily give you.

  • Interim Director of Technology / CTO
  • Independent advisory to principal & board
  • ICT team assessment, coaching & mentoring
  • Technology staff recruitment support

How I work
I become part of your team. When I work with a school, I don't arrive with a standard playbook and leave with a report. I sit alongside your leadership, understand your community, and build something that is genuinely yours — strategy, capability, culture, and all. My career started in the classroom. That perspective never leaves the room with me.
Purpose before technology

Every technology decision in a school is also an educational decision. I start with your values and your vision — and technology follows from that, not the other way around.

Honest, not reassuring

I would rather tell you something uncomfortable and useful than something polished and hollow. Good thinking partners tell you what you need to hear — not what you want to hear.

Built to last

Everything I build is designed to outlast my involvement. Strategies, frameworks, capability — all of it is documented, embedded, and owned by your team long after the engagement ends.
